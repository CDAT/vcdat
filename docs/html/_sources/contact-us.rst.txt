=================================
Contact Us
=================================

Found a bug? Have an idea that would make vCDAT better or easier to use? Let us know using one of the contact methods below!

Submit an Issue
---------------------

Submitting an issue is the most reliable way to bring attention to a bug or feature request. Please be sure to include as many details as possible. Some commonly requested pieces of information include:

* Individual steps that lead to the problem in question.
* What OS you are running
* The output of `conda list vc`
* The output of `glxinfo`

Email the Support Team
-------------------------

Send an email to cdat-support@llnl.gov
